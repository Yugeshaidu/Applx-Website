Meet the team

Ferdos Osman

Ferdos Osman is a director of Employment and education centre, with experience managing global teams. Her background in brand strategy, visual design, and account management informs her mindful but competitive approach. She is fueled by her passion for understanding the nuances of cross-cultural advertising.

She considers herself a ‘forever student,’ eager to both build on her academic foundations in psychology and sociology and stay in tune with the latest digital marketing strategies through continued coursework.

Ferdos’s hunger for knowledge and determination to turn information into action has contributed to her most recent success to the community, she vastly improved the productivity of her team by implementing strategic project management methods and ensuring a work-life balance for her department.

Ferdos believes mindfulness in the workplace is key to success - a tenet she lives out through her interests in Fashion, meditation, reading, and listening to music. Ferdos is currently working as a freelance marketing director and is always interested in a challenge.

Eric Mwamba

Eric Mwamba is a worldwide journalist. He won international acclaim for, among other stories, his reports on the Ivorian cocoa mafia, the international trade in young African football players, and his deep-digging exposure of the system of corruption that reigns in some African countries. He works on the cross border global issues such as corruption, human rights and governance.

Mwamba's work has been published in the DRC, Belgium, Germany, South Africa and the United States. He visited the Netherlands in 2013 to research a subject that is a tad unusual for him: the old Dutch tradition of Sinterklaas and his helper 'Black Pete'.

He is a media entrepreneur and community intelligence provider. As such his field of education includes international development, Communication, interactive digital media and Criminology.
