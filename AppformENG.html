SCHOLARSHIP APPLICATION

Who can Apply?

High school finalists, university and higher institutes students in the rising promotion.

Interested candidates must respect the following:

  • Only complete files will be considered for selection.
  • Only legible files are considered by the jury!
  • The decisive criterion, which will be retained by the jury when evaluating your application, is the development and potential of the project for which you intend to contribute to the construction of your country after your studies;
  • The description of the project must be detailed, considered.
  • Your project must be clearly presented.

    • Key points to highlight in your file:

  • Your personal motivation;
  • Why you developed your project?
  • The real and concrete possibility of carrying out your project;
  • How your project would contribute to the community and to your country;
  • How and where your project will be carried out.

    • Application formalities

  • Period for submitting the application file: June 6th, 2022 up to 30th, 2022
  • Applications received after June 30th, 2022, 24:00, will not be accepted.
  • Application forms are available from June 6th, 2022 both ONLINE and at the GRAND JOURNAL office, between Monday to Friday from 8:00 A.M. to 5:30 P.M.

    • You can choose to fill up the application online or offline.
