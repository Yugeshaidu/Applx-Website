Applx Membership

Join hands with us in building a global community.

Applx committee invites call people of the wise community to access the registration forms and get registered in preparation for the upcoming projects. Membership registration is required in order for a member to access the privileges to be able to vote and engage with organisation activities. The committee is tasked to open up membership registration to the dateline no later than 21st of October 2022. The membership registration fee is $50.

Requirements to register as a member:

  • Applicant must be over 18 years old.
  • Be a member of the Victorian wise community
  • Must be resident of Victoria.

    • Rights of Members:

    A member of the Applx is in entitled to vote, receive notice of general meetings and of proposed special resolutions in the manner and time prescribed by these Rules. Has right to submit items of business for consideration at a general meeting, attend and be heard at general meetings and has right to vote at a general meeting. The member has access to minutes of general meetings, other documents of the organisation and the register of members.

    Empower Youth Education

    Applx is working to advocate and build the capacity of young people in developing community overseas. The project is about to support youth from the Democratic Republic of Congo, DRC, one the African countries devasted by years of war and conflicts to be competitive on the global employment jobs. As such Applx aims to provide scholarships and small grants for small businesses.
    The project will bring together local organisation and Australians build relationships and capacity in young people of the above named country who is at risk involving lack of quality education, employment and criminality.

    To apply to become a member of Applx, the candidate has to fill and submit the application. He/she also has to pay annual membership fee. The applicant has to support the purpose of Applx. He has to agree to comply with Applx rules. One of the key rules is that the referees have not to be related to the applicant.

    You can choose to fill up the application online or offline.
