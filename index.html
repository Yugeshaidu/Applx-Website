APPLX is a not for profit organisation incorporated in Victoria Australia. It is providing information, analysis, participative inquiries, orientation and guidelines concerning issues affecting youth and small businesses fields in some developing societies including outside and within Australia. It is a neutral and independent network working with communities, internet and other experts. APPLX uses latest generation information technologies for the benefit of communities who are often deprived quality information, this enabling them to take action and face challenges concerning the problems, the various phenomena and the decisions that hinder the development of the communities. Then search for solutions within and with community members.

Applx is working to advocate and build the capacity of young people in developing community overseas. The project is about to support youth from the Democratic Republic of Congo, DRC, one the African countries devasted by years of war and conflicts to be competitive on the global employment jobs. As such Applx aims to provide scholarships and small grants for small businesses.
